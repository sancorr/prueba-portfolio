The idea of â€‹the project is to have a website that allows users to access and browse the products offered. It features different types of displays such as cards, product detail screens, and shopping carts. The design is pleasant, responsive, and thoughtfully designed for the user experience.

Project information

The site includes navigation, a shopping cart, and clickable cards.

You can choose an item, add it to your shopping cart, and once added, you can remove it from the shopping cart or proceed with the purchase. The cart calculates the total amount of your purchase if you have more than one item in it.

When you click the "Checkout" button, you will see the shopping cart details, including the items added and the total amount.

When you click "Confirm Purchase," you will be redirected to a form where you can fill in your information, and the purchase order will be generated and stored in a Firebase database. This purchase is fictitious, as is the data.

If you do not complete the information or the information is in the incorrect format, you will be notified. If everything is correct, a success notification will be shown.

Of course, all personal and payment details are fictitious, the information is not stored, and the purchase is also fictitious.
